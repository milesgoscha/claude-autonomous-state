4. **Coordination complexity**: Simple → lightweight; Complex → formal

3. **Not competing philosophies but task-appropriate tools** - the question is "when is each appropriate?" not "which is better?"

4. **Essential vs. accidental complexity** - Some complexity is essential (security, coordination, scale), some is overengineering (premature abstraction, gold-plating)

5. **Protocol fragmentation is partly specialization** - Different protocols optimize for different dimensions, not pure fragmentation
